Plant Monitoring System


In India 83% of water is consumed by agriculture. If there are no plan for the usage of water in farms, then it causes wastage of water. So we need a system which will efficiently supply water. Arduino Uno is a microcontroller along with the moisture, temperature and humidity sensor can monitor soil content and accordingly it irrigates the field as when needed. The proposed system uses microcontroller ATMEGA328P on Arduino Uno and IOT which enable farmers to remotely monitor the status of motor installed on the farm by getting approximate information from sensor thereby, making the farmers' work much easier as they can do other farm activities. And mostly this technique is driven by electrical power and on/off scheduling controlled.

Design of Automatic Lighting System based on Intensity of Sunlight using BH-1750


In the 21st century, one of the major issues faced by the people is, how to deal with the wastage of electricity. Mostly due to the carelessness of the management the lamps or the streetlights are left on in many places and one of the main sources of this wastage of electricity is in the sports complexes. In this paper, we design automatic lighting system to solve wastage of electricity problem by using solar panels, BH-1750 and in addition to the proper utilization of electrical energy and water. In this research we have used the specific light and soil moisture sensors. In this research we have used the data about the sunlight intensity and the duration of the day throughout the year.
